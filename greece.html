Greece · Zakynthos · One home

The Ionian.
A private path to the sea.

On the quiet western coast of Zakynthos — in a village that still belongs to its villagers — one house opens directly onto the Ionian. We chose this single home, alone, because it does what the rest of the island rarely lets you have: the beach, the privacy, the silence — all in the same week.

The Ionian seen from the gardens — olive and cypress in the foreground, two infinity pools below, a private stretch of beach hidden just out of frame.
